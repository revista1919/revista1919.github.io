Article Submission Policies

Editorial Protocol RevCiencias • v.2025

01. Introduction

This document establishes the regulatory framework for submitting manuscripts to the National Review of Sciences for Students. "Submission" is defined as any transfer of intellectual material through our official channels.

02. Clauses and Conditions

Regulatory Adherence Submitting the article implies full acceptance of the current Editorial Guidelines.
Data Availability The author accepts that the form information and the manuscript will be available to the journal for editorial processing and potential public publication.
Review Rights The journal is granted a non-exclusive right to evaluation, correction, feedback, and issuance of technical reports.
Editorial Discretion The submission process does not constitute a promise of publication. Final acceptance depends solely on the peer committee and editors.
Ethical Responsibility The journal commits to rigorous review; the author assumes receiving professional criticism as part of academic growth.
Manuscript Iteration There are no resubmission limits for rejected articles, as long as they incorporate significant improvements after feedback.

03. Final Notes

Non-compliance with any of these points empowers the journal to dismiss the submission immediately. Transparency and rigor are the pillars of our institution.
