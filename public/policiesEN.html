Article Submission Policies

The National Review of Sciences for Students

Introduction

This document outlines the policies for submitting articles to The National Review of Sciences for Students. The term "submission" refers to the process of sending an article through the online form available on the journal’s website, as well as any other procedures followed for this purpose.

Terms and Conditions

The following terms and conditions apply to the submission process:

  • Compliance with Editorial Guidelines
    By submitting an article, the author agrees to adhere to the editorial guidelines specified on the journal’s website, accessible at: https://www.revistacienciasestudiantes.com/en/guidelines/.
  • Data Availability
    By submitting an article, the author consents to the data provided in the submission form and the article document being fully available to the journal and used as public data on the internet.
  • Non-Exclusive Rights
    By submitting an article, the author grants the journal a non-exclusive right to read the article, make necessary corrections, provide feedback, and issue a report on the corrections made.
  • No Guarantee of Publication
    Submitting an article does not guarantee its publication. The decision to reject an article is at the discretion of the reviewers and editor, based on any reasons outlined in the editorial guidelines.
  • Responsibility for Emotional Impact
    The journal is not responsible for any emotional impact that feedback may have on the author.
  • Resubmission and Multiple Submissions
    If an article is rejected, the author may resubmit it as many times as desired. Additionally, the author may submit as many articles as they deem appropriate. However, acceptance of any specific article is not guaranteed.
  • Commitment Upon Acceptance
    If an article is accepted, the author agrees to cooperate in the publication process by providing the legal information required by the journal.

Final Notes

These policies are mandatory for all authors submitting articles to the journal. Non-compliance may result in the rejection of the submission. For further details on the editorial guidelines, please refer to the link provided in point 1.
