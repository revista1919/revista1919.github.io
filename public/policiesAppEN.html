Policies for Submitting Applications for Positions

The National Review of Sciences for Students

Introduction

This document aims to establish the policies for submitting applications for any position at The National Review of Sciences for Students. This includes sending through the online form available on the journal's website, as well as any other process followed for the submission.

Terms and Conditions

The following terms and conditions apply to the process:

  • Commitment to Position Responsibilities
    First, the applicant commits to having read the responsibilities associated with the position they are applying for through the link https://www.revistacienciasestudiantes.com/en/admin by clicking on the position title.
  • Data Availability
    Second, the applicant accepts that the data related to the form and the document itself will be fully available to the journal and used as public data on the internet. And if accepted, their professional information will be published on the website. Informing if under 13 years old, to agree with their parents on information handling.
  • Active Collaboration
    Third, the applicant commits to actively collaborate during the process, responding to emails and providing the relevant required information about data for the journal and legal processes.
  • Commitment Upon Acceptance
    Fourth, if the applicant is accepted for their position, they commit to remaining active and completing their assigned tasks, and to inform if they cannot do so.
  • Disassociation
    Any member may disassociate from the project whenever they wish.
  • No Guarantee of Acceptance
    The act of submitting an application does not imply that it will be accepted. Additionally, the applicant commits to attending the interview if preselected.

Final Notes

These policies are mandatory for all applicants. Any non-compliance may result in the rejection of the application. For more details on position responsibilities, please refer to the link provided in point 1.
