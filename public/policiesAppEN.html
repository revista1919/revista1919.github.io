Policies for Submitting Applications for Positions

Recruitment and Selection • San Felipe, Chile

01. Introduction

The National Review of Sciences for Students (ISSN 3087-2839) is governed by principles of voluntary collaboration and academic excellence. This document details the conditions for students who wish to join our administrative or editorial team, based in San Felipe, Chile.

02. Applicant Terms

Knowledge of the Position The applicant declares to have knowledge of the specific responsibilities detailed in our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.
Data Transparency If accepted, the collaborator authorizes the publication of their professional profile on the official website. Minors must have express parental authorization through the form available at /en/login.
Communication Management The applicant commits to maintaining active communication channels and responding to the requirements of the selection process in a timely manner.
Operational Commitment Acceptance of a position entails the responsibility to fulfill assigned tasks and proactively report any impediment to their execution.
Voluntary and Withdrawal Collaboration is voluntary; any member may request their disassociation at any time they deem appropriate, following task handover protocols.
Profile Evaluation The application does not guarantee incorporation. The process may include technical interviews for preselected candidates.
Right to Withdrawal The applicant may withdraw their candidacy at any time during the process by written communication to the official email.

03. Final Notes

We seek students committed to scientific dissemination. Non-compliance with these policies during the application process will invalidate the candidate's profile.
